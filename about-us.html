About Us

In the tapestry of our high school days, fate wove a tale of diverse passions and unexpected connections. It all began in the hallowed halls of Latin class, where conjugating verbs and deciphering ancient texts ignited an unspoken bond. Just as in the words of Seneca, Amor tussisque non celantur, our love, like a cough, was not hidden. As fate would have it, the anime club became our sanctuary, where shared laughter and whispered discussions deepened our connection. Amidst the vibrant tales on the screen, our limited knowledge of Japanese was dissolved by the presence of subtitles, which bridged the linguistic gap and added to the magic. We didn't understand the show, but we understood each other, and we couldn't care for anything more. Alongside these adventures, the world of rocketry united us in the pursuit of scientific exploration and engineering marvels. From Latin class to the anime club and into the boundless skies, our love story unfolded, reminding us that destiny weaves its threads in the most unexpected ways, guiding us to find love, friendship, and shared dreams in the most extraordinary of places.
